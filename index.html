About Me

Hi, my name is Chryssa Jones, and I'm an aspiring web developer. I am currently enrolled in the Full Stack Developer Boot Camp at UC San Diego, where I also work full-time as a student affairs administrator. I've been in this line of work for about 7 years now. It's a good job, but it's not what I want to spend the rest of my career doing. So, I'm pursuing a career change and this boot camp is the first step!

I love coding because it gives me an opportunity to be creative and precise, and seeing my creations unfold exactly as I want them to is really exciting! Finding and squashing bugs is a fun part of the process, like a scavenger hunt. I love the flexibility it gives me, I love being part of a team, and I'm looking forward to a new career opportunity.
